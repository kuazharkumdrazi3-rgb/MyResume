Featured Projects & Portfolio

Selected initiatives that show how I connect data, people, and process to protect quality.

SPC Implementation on Critical Parameters

From reacting to complaints to acting on trend signals.

  • Situation: Process drifts were only visible when defects reached customers.
  • What I did: Defined control charts for key parameters and trained operators and engineers.
  • Result: Earlier detection of shifts and reduced repeat deviations and rework.

Standardised Deviation & CAPA Workflow

Making investigations structured and traceable.

  • Situation: Deviation reports were inconsistent and difficult to follow.
  • What I did: Introduced a standard template with clear sections for 5-Why, risk, and action tracking.
  • Result: Faster closure and better visibility on repeated root causes.

Supplier CoA Verification Programme

Balancing trust in CoA with real incoming data.

  • Situation: Heavy reliance on supplier CoA created blind spots.
  • What I did: Set sampling plans to periodically verify CoA values against in-house tests.
  • Result: Identified trends early and used data to discuss improvements with suppliers.

Quality Training for Frontline Teams

Turning concepts into day-to-day behaviour.

  • Situation: Operators saw quality requirements as extra paperwork.
  • What I did: Designed short, practical sessions on “why” behind specs and documentation.
  • Result: Higher engagement in reporting issues and following control plans.
