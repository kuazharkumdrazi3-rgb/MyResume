Key Achievements

98–100% In-Spec Product

Consistent product quality across multiple plants.

Sustained 98–100% in-spec product conditions through SPC, stricter process controls and close collaboration between production, planning and QC.

30% Fewer Defects, 25% Fewer Complaints

From firefighting to structured problem solving.

Implemented Lean management, 7 QC tools, and RCA/CAPA frameworks that reduced non-conformances by 30%, customer complaints by 25% and rework by about 15%.

95% Audit Ratings

Customer & system audits with no major findings.

Led preparation for customer, ISO and internal audits; strengthened documentation control and training; achieved around 95% audit scores with no major non-conformances.

20% Lab Efficiency & 50% Downtime Cut

Lean lab and preventive maintenance.

Introduced Lean lab layout, clear priorities and preventive maintenance, improving lab efficiency by 20% and cutting instrument downtime by 50%, protecting release lead-times.

Supplier Quality +20% & Delivery +30%

Data-based supplier partnership.

Implemented supplier scorecards and CoA verification programmes that improved supplier defect rates by 20% and delivery performance by 30% via clearer expectations and structured feedback.
