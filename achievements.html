Key Achievements

Snapshot of the impact I’ve delivered in quality, production, and audit performance.

0 Major Audit Findings

Maintained clean external audit results.

Led readiness for external and customer audits by tightening document control, closing gaps early, and aligning production, QC, and QA on the same checklist.

Defect Rate Reduced

Defects reduced through SPC and root cause work.

Introduced routine SPC charts on critical parameters and used data to trigger cross-functional problem solving, preventing repeat issues instead of accepting rework.

Faster Deviation Closure

Shortened investigation and closure cycle time.

Standardised deviation templates, guided teams on 5-Why and fishbone analysis, and set clear expectations with production, reducing open deviations and follow-up emails.

Supplier Quality Improved

Supplier scorecards used to drive performance.

Helped implement supplier evaluation criteria and feedback loops using incoming inspection data so that supplier performance became measurable and visible.

Quality Culture Shift

From blame to prevention mindset.

Facilitated toolbox talks and trainings to reframe QA from “policing” to “partnering”, encouraging operators and engineers to raise issues early.
