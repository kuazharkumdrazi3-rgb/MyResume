Skill Tiers Overview

Based on my skill-tier model: Unlocked = already used in roles, Growing = in progress, Future = targeted for next career steps.

T1 – Inspector Unlocked

In-process & final inspection, visual checks, sampling, basic QC testing and accurate data recording.

T2 – Supervisor Unlocked

Shift/line supervision, follow-up on defects, coordinating rework, guiding operators, basic reporting and escalation.

T3 – Executive Unlocked

Documentation control, QC lab operations and cross-functional communication. Unlocked skills: Documentation Control, QC Lab Operations, Cross-functional Communication.

T4 – Engineer Unlocked

Engineering-level quality tools and system ownership. Unlocked skills: ISO 9001 QMS, Internal Auditing, Deviation & CAPA, SPC & Process Monitoring, 7 QC Tools, Data Trending & Reporting, Root Cause Analysis, FMEA/PFMEA, Continuous Improvement Projects, Coaching & Mentoring.

T5 – Manager Unlocked

Site-level quality management, strategy execution and people leadership. Unlocked skills: Quality Career Core (Inspector → Manager path), Risk-Based QMS, Advanced Data Interpretation, DoE, Team Leadership (QA/QC/Lab), Quality Culture Building.

T6 – Director-Level / Specialist

Growing: ISO 13485 / Medical Device, Strategic Quality Leadership. Future / Target: Halal Certification Management, HACCP / Food Safety, ISO 17025 Lab Accreditation, Plant-wide Quality Strategy.
